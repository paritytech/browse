Parity reviews each product against its Product Guidelines before granting this
certificate. It is revoked on the network if the product stops meeting the bar.
The standard is simple. If it feels like crypto, we have not done our job.

- **Speak human.** No "login", "connect", "wallet", or "sign". No hashes, hex, or
  raw addresses. Humans have names.
- **Declare intent, not action.** Ask for preferences and permission. Never ask
  for a technical operation or a concept the user has never met.
- **Never block the user.** Render optimistically and reconcile in the
  background. Never wait for finality without severe economic stakes.
- **Errors are ours, never theirs.** Handle them seamlessly and say what to do
  next. Never quote the chain.
- **Undo beats confirmation.** Prefer reversible actions over dialogs.
- **Design for thumbs.** Tap targets over text fields. Avoid unnecessary taps.
- **Private by default.** Cross-product activity stays unlinkable unless the user
  chooses to reveal it.
